DESCRIPTION

The orange circles represent the concentration of hate crimes based on every micro-community within Seattle. Please feel free to identify yourself with the dropdown menus and check to see your risk levels in the Seattle metropolitan area. We hope to educate the general public about the prejudice levels for every demographic and provide a safer outlook for marginalized individuals. The data is taken from the SPD database on bias/hate crime which is maintained regularly by the bias unit. The data has been repackaged and reformatted from various broken .csv files and handwritten report entries into a readable and comprehensive layout for the safety of all.
